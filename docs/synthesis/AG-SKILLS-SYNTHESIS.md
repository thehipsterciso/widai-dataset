## Overview

Domain: AI Governance & Ethics (AG)

Previous count: 19 (legacy entries, pre-evidence-first)

Methodology: Evidence-first synthesis per KSA-SYNTHESIS-METHODOLOGY.md v2.0.0

## Methodology

Built from STRM rationale evidence per KSA-SYNTHESIS-METHODOLOGY.md v2.0.0. Existing entries (19) discarded as starting point. Concepts extracted from ~980 unique framework elements at STS ≥ 0.55 across 6 frameworks, clustered into 30 distinct skill competencies, then subjected to 3 adversarial passes (coverage gaps, redundancy, domain boundary). Pass 1 identified 11 coverage gaps with strong multi-framework support; Passes 2 and 3 found no merges or removals. Final count: 30 (up from 19).
