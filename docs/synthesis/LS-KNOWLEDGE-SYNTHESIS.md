## Overview

This synthesis covers the **Knowledge dimension** of the **Leadership & Strategy (LS)** domain. The LS domain encompasses strategic planning, executive governance, organizational design, talent management, business case development, innovation, and transformation—the foundational knowledge required for leaders and strategists to guide data and AI initiatives at the organizational level.

**Evidence Density Analysis:** 260 unique framework elements at STS ≥0.60 across 6 STRM-scored frameworks, 1,318 at STS ≥0.55. Original count: 13 entries. Evidence density ratio: 7.2:1 (well below critical threshold of 10:1).
